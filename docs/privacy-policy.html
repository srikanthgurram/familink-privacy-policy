Privacy Policy for FamiLink

Last updated: January 28, 2026

FamiLink ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FamiLink mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Family member information you add (names, relationships, birth dates)
  • Photos you upload for family member profiles

1.2 Camera Permission

We request camera access to allow you to:

  • Take photos for family member profiles
  • Capture images directly within the app

Important: We only access your camera when you explicitly choose to take a photo. We do not access your camera in the background or without your permission.

1.3 Photo Library Access

We request access to your photo library to allow you to select existing photos for family member profiles. We only access photos you explicitly select.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Allow you to create and manage family trees
  • Enable collaboration with invited family members
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: For family tree data
  • Firebase Storage: For photos

All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data.

4. Data Sharing and Privacy

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your family tree data is:

  • Private by default: Only you can see your family trees
  • Shared only when you invite others: You control who has access
  • Not publicly accessible: Your data is never made public without your explicit permission

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and storage
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates

These services may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View all data we have about you
  • Delete your data: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data: Request a copy of your family tree data
  • Revoke permissions: Disable camera or photo access in your device settings at any time

7. Children's Privacy

While our app allows you to document family members of all ages, we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All family member data is entered by adult account holders.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your account information is deleted immediately
  • Family tree data and photos are deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@familink.in
  • App: FamiLink mobile application

This privacy policy was created for FamiLink and is effective as of January 28, 2026.
